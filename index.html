FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BallBrain.

What is BallBrain?

BallBrain is an AI-powered basketball training and nutrition platform that creates personalized workout plans and meal plans tailored to your position, body type, and goals.

How does the AI personalization work?

Our AI analyzes your role, body measurements, fitness level, and goals to create a custom 6-week program with daily workouts and meal plans optimized for basketball performance.

What equipment do I need?

Most workouts can be done at home or in a gym with minimal equipment. We provide variations for different settings and adapt exercises based on what's available to you.

How long are the workouts?

Each session is designed to be 30 minutes, including warm-up and cool-down. Perfect for busy schedules while ensuring effective training.

Are the meal plans customized?

Yes! Our meal plans are tailored to your goals, local foods available in your region, and dietary preferences. Everything is timed for optimal performance and recovery.

Can I track my progress?

Absolutely! Track your workouts, monitor your stats, build streaks, earn achievements, and see your improvement over time with our comprehensive analytics.
